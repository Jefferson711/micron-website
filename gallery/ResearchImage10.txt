Cell division . Mouse myoblast cell in late stage of cell division imaged with 3D-SIM. Microtubules (yellow) and the reconstituting nuclear lamina (red) are immunolabelled with anti-tublin and anti-lamin B antibodies, respectively. Chromatin (blue) is stained with DAPI. Projection view of a 3D image stack (Lothar Schermelleh).
