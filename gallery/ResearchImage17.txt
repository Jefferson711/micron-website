Neuromuscular junction from Drosophila larva acquired on a Perkin-Elmer spinning disk confocal. Axon terminals (branched, white structures) and a nuclear protein (round, white structures) were labelled by immunofluorescence. Dlg mRNA (red punctate in the nuclei) was detected with FISH. Max projection of a z-stack. (Josh Titlow. Davis lab).
