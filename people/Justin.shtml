Justin Demmerle - Schermelleh Group

email:justin.demmerle@bioch.ox.ac.uk

Justin joined the University of Oxford in January 2013 from the University of Chicago, and has a background in chromatin architecture, the nuclear lamina, and fluorescence in-situ hybridization (FISH) experiments. He is interested in using advanced light microscopy techniques, particularly SIM, to investigate the spatial dynamics of chromatin folding, gene positioning, and gene expression. He enjoys imaging things that nobody has seen before, and relaxing in Fourier space.
